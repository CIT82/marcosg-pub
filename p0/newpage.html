Gun Safety Class Policies & Rules

General Policies

  • Participants must be at least 18 years old or accompanied by a legal guardian.
  • All attendees must complete a waiver before participation.
  • Class fees must be paid in full before the session begins.
  • Attendees must follow all instructor directions at all times.

Safety Rules

  • All firearms must be unloaded and in a secure case upon arrival.
  • Finger off the trigger until ready to fire.
  • Always keep the firearm pointed in a safe direction.
  • Hearing and eye protection must be worn at all times on the range.

Code of Conduct

  • Respect all participants and instructors.
  • Reckless behavior will result in immediate removal from class.
  • No alcohol or drugs before or during the session.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Cancelations made 48 hours in advance will receive a full refund.
  • No-shows or late cancellations are non-refundable.
