Performance

The Tesla Semi is a revolutionary electric truck designed to transform freight transport. With its all-electric powertrain, the Semi offers unparalleled performance, efficiency, and safety for long-haul trucking. Its advanced autopilot system ensures safe operation, while the zero-emissions design reduces environmental impact. Offering a range of up to 500 miles on a single charge, the Tesla Semi is equipped with state-of-the-art technology for both the driver and the cargo. Experience the future of freight transportation with Tesla's Semi and reduce costs while improving your environmental footprint.

  • Range: 300-500 miles
  • Full Autonomous Driving
  • Heavy Duty: Designed for Maximum Payload
  • Zero-Emission, Sustainable Powertrain
  • Improved Safety with Advanced Autopilot Features
